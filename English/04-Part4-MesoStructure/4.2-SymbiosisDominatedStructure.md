# Chapter 10 Symbiosis-Dominated Structure

## 10.1 Core Logic: Actively Designed Sustainable Form

### Core Proposition

**The Symbiosis-Dominated Structure is a sustainable development structure actively designed and constructed by humans after recognizing the objective laws of social operation, with the core goal of maximizing the long-term survival and evolutionary development of the group as a whole.**

### Design Principles

1. **Systemic Principle**: Design structure from the perspective of the whole system, rather than local optimization.
2. **Fairness Principle**: Ensure opportunity fairness in resource allocation, eliminate institutionalized predation.
3. **Efficiency Principle**: Improve resource utilization efficiency through reasonable division of labor and collaboration.
4. **Sustainability Principle**: Ensure long-term stability and evolutionary ability of the system.
5. **Adaptability Principle**: Able to self-adjust and optimize with environmental changes.

### Operating Logic

- **Bidirectional Resource Circulation**: Surplus resources form a positive cycle within the system, feeding back to the production layer.
- **Open and Transparent Rules**: Resource allocation rules are open and transparent, accepting supervision.
- **Power Checks and Balances**: Achieve effective checks and balances of power through institutional design.
- **Innovation Incentive**: Establish mechanisms to encourage innovation, releasing individual vitality.

## 10.2 Key Characteristics: Anti-Predation and Positive Resource Circulation

### 1. Zero Institutionalized Predation

- **Core Characteristic**: With public ownership of means of production as the main body and multiple forms of ownership developing together as the core, abolish gratuitous predation based on monopoly of means of production and power from the root.
- **Implementation**:
  - Property Rights Reform: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production.
  - Distribution System Design: Implement distribution according to work as the main body, with multiple distribution methods coexisting.
  - Supervision Mechanism: Construct a diversified rigid supervision system to regulate power operation.
- **Result**: Individual/Group income is only linked to labor contribution, management value, technological innovation, and risk assumption, completely closing the institutional channel of getting something for nothing.

### 2. Bidirectional Positive Circulation of Resource Flow

- **Core Characteristic**: Surplus resources created by the production layer no longer flow unidirectionally to the top enrichment layer, but flow back to the public domain through the public distribution system.
- **Flow Path**:
  - Production layer creates surplus resources.
  - Part of surplus resources used for public undertakings (education, medical care, infrastructure, innovation).
  - Public undertakings feed back to production layer, improving production efficiency.
  - Form a positive closed loop of "Production-Distribution-Feedback-Reproduction".
- **Result**: All members share development results, system achieves sustainable development.

### 3. Opportunity Fairness in Class Mobility

- **Core Characteristic**: The only standard for individual level leap is labor contribution, ability level, and innovation results, completely unrelated to birth, intergenerational resources, and blood background.
- **Implementation**:
  - Education Equity: Ensure fair distribution of educational resources.
  - Employment Fairness: Eliminate employment discrimination, establish fair selection mechanism.
  - Social Security: Establish social assistance and safety net mechanisms.
  - Entrepreneurship Support: Provide entrepreneurship opportunities and resources for bottom groups.
- **Result**: Class mobility is smooth, social vitality is fully released.

### 4. Service-Oriented Reconstruction of Level Functions

- **Core Characteristic**: The core functions of the Enrichment Layer and Transmission Layer completely transform from "Rule Making and Resource Predation" to "Serving Group Development and Bearing System Risks".
- **Implementation**:
  - Role Repositioning: Clarify service functions of each level.
  - Incentive Mechanism Adjustment: Establish incentive system linked to service performance.
  - Supervision System Perfection: Ensure power operates in the sun.
- **Result**: Transmission Layer returns to the core function of "Resource Regulation Valve", Enrichment Layer becomes the system's "Risk Bearer" and "Innovation Leader".

### 5. Dynamic Balance of Efficiency and Fairness

- **Core Characteristic**: Do not engage in egalitarianism, clearly protect reasonable income differences based on labor, management, innovation, and risk assumption, while curbing polarization through tax regulation and social security mechanisms.
- **Implementation**:
  - Primary Distribution: Focus on efficiency, encourage innovation and contribution.
  - Redistribution: Focus on fairness, regulate income gap through taxation.
  - Tertiary Distribution: Encourage philanthropy, promote social harmony.
- **Result**: Achieve dynamic unity of "Fairness Safety Net, Efficiency Improvement".

## 10.3 Implementation Conditions: Institutional Design and Mechanism Guarantee

### 1. Property Rights System

- **Core Requirement**: Clarify public ownership or fair distribution of means of production, eliminate monopoly possession by private or small groups.
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Public Ownership as Main Body: Implement public ownership in important resources and key areas.
  - Coexistence of Multiple Ownerships: Encourage healthy development of non-public economy.
  - Property Rights Protection: Protect various legal property rights according to law.

### 2. Supervision Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Construct a diversified rigid supervision system of "National Supervision + Internal Self-Reform + Third-Party Assessment".
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Democratic Supervision: Expand citizen participation, enhance breadth of supervision.
  - Institutional Supervision: Establish and improve power restriction and supervision mechanisms.
  - Public Opinion Supervision: Give play to the supervision role of media and public opinion.
  - Self-Supervision: Establish internal self-correction mechanism of the system.

### 3. Distribution System

- **Core Requirement**: Implement "Distribution according to work as the main body, multiple distribution methods coexisting", balancing efficiency and fairness.
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Labor Remuneration: Ensure labor remuneration matches labor contribution.
  - Factor Participation in Distribution: Allow capital, technology, management and other factors to participate in distribution.
  - Social Security: Establish social security system covering all people.
  - Tax Regulation: Regulate excessive income through progressive tax and other methods.

### 4. Mobility Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Promote education equity, employment fairness, establish and improve social assistance and upward mobility channels.
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Education Reform: Promote balanced distribution of educational resources, guarantee education fairness.
  - Employment Service: Establish fair employment market, eliminate employment discrimination.
  - Social Assistance: Establish multi-level social assistance system.
  - Entrepreneurship Support: Provide funds, technology, policy support for entrepreneurs.

### 5. Innovation Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Increase investment in basic research and technological innovation, improve innovation achievement transformation mechanism.
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Science and Technology Investment: Increase R&D funding, support basic research.
  - Talent Cultivation: Establish innovative talent cultivation system.
  - Achievement Transformation: Improve scientific and technological achievement transformation mechanism, promote combination of production, education and research.
  - Innovation Incentive: Establish and improve innovation incentive mechanism, protect intellectual property rights.

### 6. Cultural Mechanism

- **Core Requirement**: Cultivate cultural values centered on symbiosis, cooperation, and innovation.
- **Specific Measures**:
  - Value Guidance: Advocate collectivism, cooperative spirit, and innovation consciousness.
  - Education Cultivation: Integrate symbiosis concept into education system.
  - Cultural Inheritance: Inherit and innovate excellent traditional culture.
  - Public Opinion Guidance: Create social atmosphere conducive to symbiotic development.
