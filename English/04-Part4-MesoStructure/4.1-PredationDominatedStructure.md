# Chapter 9 Predation-Dominated Structure: The Gravity of Entropy Increase

## 9.1 Core Definition: The Default Option of the System

**Predation-Dominated Structure** is not some evil design, but **the default physical form of human society in its natural state**.

According to the **Axiom of Energy Least Resistance Path**: In the absence of reverse work (institutional constraints), the system always tends to slide towards the state of maximum entropy increase—that is, obtaining energy through **predation** rather than production.

Just as water flows downwards, without a water pump (Higher Order Tao), resources inevitably flow to those who possess violence and rule-making power.

---

## 9.2 Operating Mechanism: A Precise Water Pump

The predation-dominated structure is essentially a **"Negative Entropy Water Pump"**. It continuously pumps survival resources from the bottom to the top through three subsystems.

### 1. Violence Pump (Base Layer)
- **Principle**: Directly use coercive force (army, police, prison) to guarantee the unidirectional flow of resources.
- **Function**: Ensure that the bottom layer dares not intercept the energy flow through violent resistance.
- **Energy Consumption**: High. If relying solely on violence, the cost of ruling is extremely high (Qin Dynasty mode), and the system is extremely unstable.

### 2. Institutional Pump (Middle Layer)
- **Principle**: Legitimizing and concealing predation through laws, taxes, and financial rules.
- **Common Forms**:
    - **Land Annexation/Rent**: Sitting on means of production, possessing surplus value for free.
    - **Financial Seigniorage**: Diluting bottom wealth through inflation.
    - **Franchise Rights**: Obtaining excess profits through administrative monopoly (salt and iron monopoly).
- **Function**: Reduce the friction coefficient of predation, making the plundered think "rules are rules".

### 3. Meme Pump (Top Layer)
- **Principle**: Implanting **"Obedience Memes"** through culture, religion, and education.
- **Common Rhetoric**:
    - "Divine Right of Kings" / "Mandate of Heaven"
    - "Endure the hardest hardships to become a superior person"
    - "Your poverty is because you don't work hard"
- **Function**: Make batteries fall in love with the battery compartment. This is the highest level of predation, with the lowest energy consumption and the most lasting effect.

## 9.3 Stabilizer of the System: Placebo Channel

To prevent the bottom energy from being drained causing system explosion (revolution), the predation-dominated structure designs ingenious **pressure relief valves**:

### 1. Extremely Low Probability Ascending Channel (Imperial Examination/Lottery)
- **Mechanism**: Allow extremely few bottom individuals (0.01%) to become top layer through imperial examinations, college entrance exams, or entrepreneurship.
- **Function**:
    - **Extract Elites**: Absorb the smartest and most resistant individuals from the bottom into the ruling group.
    - **Create Hope**: Make the remaining 99.99% think "I didn't succeed because I'm not capable", rather than "the system has problems".

### 2. Tittytainment
- **Mechanism**: Provide cheap entertainment (colosseum, short videos, soap operas).
- **Function**: Consume excess energy (physiological energy) and emotions (psychological energy) of the bottom, preventing them from transforming into thinking and resistance.

## 9.4 Evolutionary Fate: Thermodynamic Collapse

Despite designing layers of stability mechanisms, the predation-dominated structure cannot escape the **"Historical Cycle Law"**. This is not because the emperor is incompetent, but because of **physical laws**.

### Collapse Process

1.  **Initial Stage (Establishment)**: The new regime resets resource allocation through violence (killing the rich to aid the poor), the parasitic coefficient $Pc$ is low, and the system quickly accumulates negative entropy through "recuperation".
2.  **Middle Stage (Enrichment)**: The enrichment layer uses rule advantages to continuously increase pumping intensity. The transmission layer (bureaucracy) begins to expand and intercept energy. $Pc$ gradually rises.
3.  **Late Stage (Rigidity)**:
    - The top layer annexes the vast majority of land/capital.
    - The middle layer intercepts the vast majority of taxes.
    - Energy input of the production layer (farmers/workers) < minimum consumption for survival.
4.  **Final Stage (Heat Death/Explosion)**:
    - **Heat Death**: If the bottom is obedient, the system enters a stagnant state, population plummets, and civilization stagnates (e.g., Wei, Jin, Northern and Southern Dynasties).
    - **Explosion**: If the bottom resists, the system undergoes violent dissipation (peasant uprising), and energy is reshuffled.

### Conclusion
**The predation-dominated structure has no future.**
It either reincarnates in cyclical collapses or rots in long-term stagnation.
Unless—introducing **Anti-Entropy Work**, leaping to the Higher Order Tao.
