# Human Trophic Theory: Underlying Logic of Human Social Evolution (One-Page Summary)

> **Core Axiom**: The universe tends towards entropy increase (disorder), and life is a dissipative structure that resists entropy (order). All institutions, cultures, and conflicts in human society are essentially for **competing for negative entropy (resources/energy)** to maintain survival order.

---

### I. Micro Mechanism: What is Human?
**Conclusion: Humans are dual slaves of "Genes" and "Memes".**
1.  **Gene Coding (Biological Instinct)**: Drives you to survive, reproduce, and pursue pleasure.
2.  **Meme Coding (Cultural Program)**: "Software" implanted in your brain through language, education, and media.
    *   **Meme Domestication**: Society uses concepts like "morality", "ideal", and "success" to transform your biological energy into group order (e.g., using "patriotism" to make individuals sacrifice, using "consumerism" to make individuals work).
    *   **False Subjectivity**: You think you are thinking, but it is actually memes thinking through your brain.

### II. Meso Structure: What is Society?
**Conclusion: Society is a "Pyramid" ecosystem of energy flow.**
Social structure is not artificially designed, but **emerged** from the games of countless individuals into three **Trophic Levels**:
1.  **Production Layer (Bottom)**: Directly creates value, but surplus value is stripped (e.g., farmers, workers).
2.  **Transmission Layer (Middle)**: Executes rules, maintains order, intercepts part of resources (e.g., bureaucrats, managers).
3.  **Accumulation Layer (Top)**: Formulates rules, legally enriches resources through institutional design (e.g., capitalists, ruling class).
*   **Energy Flow**: Resources naturally flow from the production layer to the accumulation layer, just as energy flows unidirectionally in the food chain.

### III. Macro Law: Why Does History Cycle?
**Conclusion: Lower Order Tao = Historical Cycle Law (Inevitability of Entropy Increase).**
1.  **Establishment Period**: New institutions reduce social entropy (disorder) and release productivity.
2.  **Enrichment Period**: The accumulation layer uses rule advantages to continuously absorb bottom resources (land annexation/wealth gap).
3.  **Rigidity Period**: The accumulation layer forms interest groups, hindering reform; bottom energy is exhausted, system entropy (public resentment/chaos) surges.
4.  **Collapse Period**: The system cannot maintain order, violent dissipation occurs (war/revolution), energy is redistributed, entering the next cycle.

### IV. Ultimate Way Out: Higher Order Tao
**Conclusion: Break the cycle and achieve civilization upgrade.**
1.  **Cognitive Awakening (Lingshi)**: See through meme domestication, identify the truth of energy predation behind surface narratives (like "for your own good").
2.  **Structural Reconstruction**: Shift from "Predatory Structure" (unidirectional blood-sucking) to **"Symbiotic Structure"** (bidirectional nourishment, such as open source communities, cooperatives).
3.  **Counter Entropy Increase**: Through technological innovation (making the cake bigger) and institutional justice (dividing the cake well), continuously introduce negative entropy to avoid system heat death.
