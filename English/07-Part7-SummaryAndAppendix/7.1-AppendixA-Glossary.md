## I. Cornerstone Concepts (Physics and Philosophy)

### Entropy Increase
*   **Academic Definition**: The universal trend described by the Second Law of Thermodynamics, where disorder (entropy) always tends to increase in a closed system, eventually leading to heat death.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"The Universe's Demolition Team"**. Without maintenance, rooms get messy, hot water gets cold, people age, and dynasties collapse. Chaos is the norm of the universe, order is a special state that requires energy consumption to maintain.

### Dissipative Structure
*   **Academic Definition**: An open system far from equilibrium that maintains its orderly structure by continuously taking in negative entropy (matter, energy, information) from the outside and discharging positive entropy.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Life Rowing Against the Current"**. Life, cities, and countries are all dissipative structures. They are like a vortex that must constantly eat energy and excrete waste to maintain shape. Once input stops (starvation/power cut), they fall apart (death).

### Negative Entropy
*   **Academic Definition**: A concept proposed by Schrödinger, referring to energy or information capable of reducing system entropy (increasing order).
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Life-Extending Potion"**. For humans, it's food; for civilization, it's oil, technology, and new ideas.

## III. Meso Structure (Social Trophic Levels)

### Social Trophic Level
*   **Academic Definition**: Social stratification structure formed based on energy (resource) flow direction. Status depends on the individual's position in the resource flow chain (production, transmission, or accumulation).
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Food Chain Position"**. Are you eating grass (Production Layer), eating meat (Accumulation Layer), or helping transport grass to meat-eaters (Transmission Layer)?

### Production Layer (First Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Groups that directly create negative entropy (value) but have net energy output.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Battery"**. Farmers, workers, ordinary white-collar workers. Do the most work, get the least, the cornerstone maintaining system operation.

### Transmission Layer (Second Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Intermediate groups responsible for executing rules, maintaining order, controlling energy flow rate, and intercepting part of resources.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Pipes and Valves"**. Managers, middle cadres, clerks. Do not produce directly, but manage producers, incidentally collecting tolls.

### Accumulation Layer (Third Trophic Level)
*   **Academic Definition**: Top groups formulating rules and legally accumulating the largest proportion of surplus resources.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Harvesters"**. Big capitalists, top dignitaries. They design game rules so resources automatically flow into their pockets.

### Parasitism Coefficient (Pc)
*   **Academic Definition**: Ratio of total energy consumed by Accumulation and Transmission Layers to surplus energy created by Production Layer.
*   **💡 Layman's Understanding**: **"Blood-Sucking Index"**. The higher this number, the more idlers are supported, and the more tired workers are. Exceeding 1.0, the system collapses.
