# Appendix B: Summary of Axiom System

## I. Meta-Axioms (Underlying Laws of Universe and Life)

### Axiom 1: Entropy Increase Background Axiom
**The universe as a whole tends towards disorder. Life, as an ordered structure, must continuously obtain resources from the outside to maintain survival. This is the foundation of all life behaviors.**
- **Physical Basis**: Second Law of Thermodynamics
- **Social Significance**: Social systems, as dissipative structures, require continuous resource supply.

### Axiom 2: Finite Spacetime Resources Axiom
**Within a specific time and space, the natural resources directly available to humans have an upper limit; technology can continuously expand resource boundaries, but cannot break through the instantaneous spacetime limit.**
- **Economic Significance**: Resource scarcity is the fundamental driver of social distribution games.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Resource competition is the core driving force of group evolution.

### Axiom 3: Group Evolution Priority Axiom
**The core unit of life evolution is the gene group (sharing genetic basis and cultural rules). The long-term survival and security of the group take priority over individual short-term interests.**
- **Biological Basis**: Group Selection Theory
- **Social Significance**: The ultimate driver of individual behavior is the survival of the gene group.

### Axiom 4: Dual Inheritance System Axiom
**Humans rely on two systems for inheritance: genes determine physiological instincts (evolution extremely slow); memes (institutions, technology, morality, concepts) determine social rules (evolution extremely fast), and memes directly determine group fate.**
- **Biological Significance**: Gene-Meme Co-evolution
- **Social Significance**: Cultural memes are the operating system of the social system.

## II. Social Evolution Axioms (Laws Exclusive to Human Society)

### Axiom 5: Collaborative Value-Add Axiom
**Through division of labor, collaboration, and innovation, humans can create incremental resources far greater than the simple sum of individuals. Society is not just about stock competition.**
- **Economic Significance**: Division of labor and collaboration improve production efficiency.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Collaboration is the source of group advantage.

### Axiom 6: Predation for Profit Axiom
**In a spontaneous order without global constraints, some individuals will tend to use monopoly resources and control power to possess others' labor results for free, because the cost of predation is lower than direct production.**
- **Behavioral Basis**: Rational Profit-Seeking Principle
- **Social Significance**: Predation is the inevitable tendency of spontaneous order.

### Axiom 7: Meme Legitimization Axiom
**Groups mastering rule-making power will use morality, institutions, and ideology to package their predatory behaviors as reasonable, inevitable, and legitimate rules, reducing the cost of ruling and stability maintenance.**
- **Sociological Significance**: Social control function of ideology.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Meme domestication is a necessary mechanism for system stability.

## III. Social Trophic Level Theory Axioms

### Axiom 8: Dissipative Structure Survival Axiom
**Human society is a typical dissipative system, which must continuously obtain survival and development resources from the outside, offsetting the spontaneous disorder trend within the system through resource consumption.**
- **Physical Basis**: Dissipative Structure Theory
- **Social Significance**: Resource acquisition and efficient utilization are the first prerequisite for social survival.

### Axiom 9: Resource Scarcity Axiom
**In a closed system of specific spacetime, the total amount of social available survival and development resources has a rigid upper limit.**
- **Economic Basis**: Resource scarcity is the basic assumption of economics.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Resource competition is the fundamental cause of social stratification.

### Axiom 10: Rational Profit-Seeking Axiom
**When there is no global rigid institutional constraint, individuals/groups mastering resource and power advantages will prioritize the behavioral pattern of "obtaining maximum benefits with minimum cost".**
- **Behavioral Basis**: Rational Man Assumption
- **Social Significance**: Predation is the inevitable tendency of spontaneous order.

### Axiom 11: Natural Enrichment of Surplus Resources Axiom
**The only native source of social survival and development resources is the labor creation of the bottom production group. In a spontaneous state without external rigid constraints, surplus resources naturally have a structural tendency to flow from the "production end" to the "rule-making end".**
- **Economic Basis**: Surplus Value Theory
- **Social Significance**: The directionality of resource flow is the direct cause of social stratification.

## IV. Gene Group Evolution Theory Axioms

### Axiom 12: Dual Coding Essence Axiom
**Humans are life continuation systems jointly constituted and co-evolved by genes and cultural memes.**
- **Gene Layer**: Setting underlying biological instincts (five major desires), providing system energy supply and collaboration prototype.
- **Meme Layer**: Constructing super-biological adaptation system (rules, institutions, culture), expanding survival strategy space.
- **Synergy**: Genes provide material basis for meme evolution, memes reverse shape gene expression.

### Axiom 13: Extended Phenotype Principle
**Social systems are the "extended body" constructed by gene groups in the cultural layer to maximize their own fitness.**
- **Biological Origin**: Proposed by Dawkins in "The Selfish Gene".
- **Social Significance**: Social systems are the collective adaptation device of gene groups.

### Axiom 14: Negative Entropy Drive Logic
**The stability (low entropy) of social systems depends on continuously consuming individual economic, physiological, psychological, and information energy.**
- **Thermodynamic Basis**: Dissipative Structure Theory
- **Social Significance**: System maintenance requires energy input.

### Axiom 15: Meme Domestication Mechanism
**The system transforms discrete individual energy into group entropy reduction (order) through meme domestication.**
- **Core Mechanism**: Selective redundancy, semantic anchoring, false subjectivity.
- **Social Significance**: Deep mechanism of non-violent social control.

### Axiom 16: Dynastic Cycle Law Destiny
**Stability-maintenance systems follow the dynamic destiny of "Formation (Entropy Reduction) → Stability (Low Entropy) → Maturity (Lowest Entropy) → Rigidity (Entropy Increase) → Collapse (Entropy Explosion)".**
- **System Dynamics**: Entropy Change Law
- **Historical Significance**: Explaining the underlying logic of dynastic rise and fall.

### Axiom 17: Infinite Evolution Conditions
**Breaking through the cycle law must simultaneously meet five conditions: Infinite Energy Flow, Information Openness, Meme Update, Power Dispersion, and Self-Repair.**
- **System Science**: Complex Adaptive System Theory
- **Future Significance**: Necessary conditions for continuous evolution of civilization.
