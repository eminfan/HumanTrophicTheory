# Appendix F: Methodology

This project adopts a unique **"Physics-Biology-Society" interdisciplinary reductionism** method, whose core lies in reducing human social phenomena to underlying physical and biological laws, and drawing conclusions through strict logical deduction.

This methodology is called **"Tao Analysis Method"**, and its operating mechanism is as follows:

### I. Core Research Methods

The research method of this project can be summarized as the dual path of **"Axiomatic Deduction + Lingshi Deconstruction"**:

#### 1. Axiomatic Deduction
This is the cornerstone of theoretical construction. The project does not rely on traditional sociological observation or questionnaires, but starts from **unshakable natural science axioms** and conducts logical deduction.

*   **Meta-Starting Point**: Based on **Second Law of Thermodynamics (Entropy Increase)** and **Evolutionary Biology**.
    *   **Axiom 1: Entropy Increase Irreversible** — The universe generally tends towards disorder, survival requires continuous acquisition of energy (negative entropy).
    *   **Axiom 2: Resource Finiteness** — Negative entropy flow (resources) is limited in specific time and space, leading to zero-sum games.
    *   **Axiom 3: Evolutionary Selection** — Only structures that can effectively acquire resources and reproduce can survive.
*   **Deduction Logic**:
    *   Survival needs negative entropy $\rightarrow$ Individuals must compete for resources.
    *   Individual strength is limited $\rightarrow$ Must form groups (collaborative value-add).
    *   Collaboration requires rules $\rightarrow$ Emergence of "memes" (culture/institutions) to constrain instincts.
    *   Human nature seeks advantages and avoids disadvantages $\rightarrow$ Those mastering rules inevitably tend to plunder (social stratification).
    *   **Conclusion**: Social classes, moral laws, and state machines are not artificially designed, but **inevitable mathematical solutions** of system evolution.

#### 2. Lingshi Framework (Systemic Optical Decryption)
This is a **"Cognitive Deconstruction Tool"** used to analyze specific social phenomena, which is strictly enforced in the project. It requires that when analyzing any phenomenon, surface moral narratives must be stripped away to reach the underlying interest structure.

*   **Structural Deconstruction**: Breaking down social phenomena into **Production Layer, Transmission Layer, Accumulation Layer** (Social Trophic Level Theory).
*   **Energy Tracking (Follow the Energy)**: Not looking at what people "say" (surface narrative), only looking at where resources/energy "flow".
*   **Semantic De-anchoring**: Questioning and stripping away the hypnotic effects of words like "for your own good" and "overall situation", restoring their essence as **"Meme Domestication Tools"**.
*   **Incentive Back-deduction**: Inferring the true intention of institutional design through "who ultimately benefits".

### II. How to Acquire Knowledge and Obtain Conclusions

This project acquires knowledge and verifies conclusions through the following **closed-loop process**:

1.  **Establishing Axiom System (Origin)**
    *   Establishing physics (thermodynamics) and biology (evolutionism) as unquestionable meta-starting points.
    *   Rejecting any a priori assumptions such as "natural rights" or "human nature is good".

2.  **Model Building (Modeling)**
    *   Building **"Gene-Meme Dual Coding"** model: Humans are hosts of genes (biological instincts) and memes (cultural software).
    *   Building **"Social Trophic Level"** model: Society is a pyramid structure of energy flow.

3.  **Logical Deduction (Deduction)**
    *   Under the premise of limited resources, deducing inevitable patterns of group behavior.
    *   For example: Deducing that **"Historical Cycle Law"** is not an accident, but an inevitable physical process of dissipative structure collapse caused by system entropy accumulation.

4.  **Empirical Verification (Verification)**
    *   Using **historical cases** (such as Qing Dynasty rule, Japanese militarism) and **contemporary phenomena** (such as American happy education, Soviet planned economy) for verification.
    *   Verification Standard: Does the theoretically deduced "energy flow direction" match historical facts?
    *   **Case**: When analyzing "happy education", not looking at its "respecting nature" propaganda, but looking at its results—public education reduced the ability of the bottom layer to acquire high-quality educational resources through "burden reduction", thereby solidifying classes (energy flows to accumulation layer), verifying the "Class Solidification Theorem".

5.  **Conclusion Generation (Conclusion)**
    *   Conclusions are often **counter-intuitive** and **de-moralized**.
    *   For example: Morality is not sacred truth, but an **"algorithm"** to reduce internal collaboration costs; the state is not a community, but a tool for class rule (in Lower Order Tao).

### Summary
The research method of this project is **"examining human society with the cold eye of physics"**. It does not ask "what the world should be like" (ought), only "what the world must be like under physical and biological laws" (is). Ensuring logical rigor through **axiomatic deduction**, and penetrating reality through **Lingshi deconstruction**.
