# Chapter 2 General Theory: Universal Laws and Human Evolution

## 2.1 Meta-Axioms: Underlying Laws of the Universe and Life

Meta-axioms are the logical starting points of the theory, based on interdisciplinary consensus knowledge, self-evident and uncontroversial:

### Axiom 1: Dissipative Structure Axiom

The universe as a whole tends towards entropy increase (disorder), while life and social systems as local negative entropy bodies (order) are essentially **dissipative structures**. The system must continuously obtain **negative entropy flow** (matter, energy, information) from the outside and discharge **positive entropy** (waste, heat, chaos) to the outside to maintain its orderly structure.

- **Physical Basis**: Prigogine's Dissipative Structure Theory
- **Evolutionary Corollary**: Once negative entropy input is insufficient or internal entropy discharge is blocked, the system will inevitably move towards disintegration (death/collapse).

### Axiom 2: Finite Spacetime Resources Axiom

Within a specific spacetime, the negative entropy flow (resources) provided by the environment has a hard upper limit. Although technology can expand resource boundaries, it can never break through instantaneous physical limits.

- **Economic Significance**: Scarcity is eternal, competition is inevitable.
- **Game Corollary**: In the stage of resource stock game, zero-sum game is the default strategy.

### Axiom 3: Group Survival Priority Axiom

The basic unit of evolution is the **Gene-Meme Complex** (Group). In long-term evolution, the survival weight of the group is higher than the survival weight of the individual. Individual behaviors that cannot maintain group survival will eventually be eliminated by evolution.

- **Biological Basis**: Group Selection Theory
- **Ethical Corollary**: The essence of "good" is beneficial to group survival, and the essence of "evil" is harmful to group survival.

### Axiom 4: Dual Inheritance System Axiom

Humans rely on two systems for inheritance: genes determine physiological instincts (evolution is extremely slow); memes (institutions, technology, morality, ideas) determine social rules (evolution is extremely fast), and memes directly determine group fate.

- **Biological Significance**: Gene-Meme Co-evolution
- **Social Significance**: Cultural memes are the operating system of the social system.

## 2.2 Social Evolution Axioms: Laws Exclusive to Human Society

### Axiom 5: Collaborative Value-Add Axiom

Through division of labor, collaboration, and innovation, humans can create **incremental resources** far greater than the simple sum of individuals. Society is not just about stock competition.

- **Economic Significance**: Division of labor and collaboration improve production efficiency.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: Collaboration is the source of group advantage.

### Axiom 6: Energy Least Resistance Path Axiom (Predation for Profit)

In a system without strong constraints, energy always flows along the path of least resistance.

- **Predation Path**: The energy consumption of directly possessing existing resources (predation) is usually lower than creating resources from scratch (production).
- **Corollary**: If there are no institutional constraints, predation is **a thermodynamic necessity**.
- **Social Phenomenon**: As long as return > risk, power rent-seeking, capital monopoly, and violent robbery will spontaneously emerge.

### Axiom 7: Meme Legitimization Axiom (Narrative Disguise)

In order to reduce the system friction (resistance cost) generated by predation, the beneficiary group will inevitably evolve a set of **memetic narratives** (morality, law, mythology) to package predation behavior as "justice" or "destiny".

- **Function**: This is the **lubricant** of the system, reducing energy internal friction by reducing cognitive conflict.
- **Evolutionary Significance**: The more successful the predation structure, the more perfect its legitimization narrative (even deceiving the predators themselves).

## 2.3 Two Evolutionary Paths: Lower Order Tao and Higher Order Tao

Based on the above axioms, there are two fundamentally different evolutionary paths in human society:

### Lower Order Tao: Spontaneously Formed Predatory Order

Lower Order Tao is **an instinctual order without top-level design or constraints**, which has been the mainstream state of human civilization for thousands of years.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Pyramid Structure**: A layered structure with many bottom-level producers and few top-level controllers.
2. **Class Solidification**: Power, resources, and advantages are transferred across generations.
3. **Placebo Channel**: Retaining an extremely small probability of upward mobility to appease the bottom layer.
4. **Moral Instrumentalization**: Morality serves the stability of the predation system.
5. **Technological Efficiency Does Not Change Essence**: The increment created by technology is prioritized and monopolized by the top layer.
6. **Endogenous Cycle Law**: Predation imbalance → Collapse → Reconstruction cycle.

#### Evolutionary Fate

- **Cyclical Collapse**: Predation intensity breaks through the survival critical line of the production layer, and the system collapses and reconstructs.
- **Stagnant Steady State**: Predation intensity is controlled below the survival critical line, forming a low-level steady state.

### Higher Order Tao: Symbiotic Order of Reverse Entropy Work

Higher Order Tao is not "natural" goodness, but **an anti-instinct, high-energy-consuming civilization reverse entropy project**. It is the group actively countering the thermodynamic tendency of "predation for profit" through precise institutional design for long-term survival.

#### Core Characteristics

1. **Anti-Entropy Design**: Must continuously invest energy (supervision, education, transparency) to maintain a "non-predatory" state.
2. **Negative Feedback Regulation**: When the wealth gap (entropy) exceeds a threshold, automatic regulation mechanisms (such as progressive tax, welfare system) are triggered.
3. **Symbiotic Structure**: Ensuring that the surplus value of the production layer is mainly used for reproduction and innovation, rather than being recklessly squandered by the top layer.

#### Evolutionary Advantage (Civilization Filter)

There may be countless civilizations in the universe, and the Lower Order Tao is a huge **Great Filter**.
Most civilizations die from internal entropy increase (collapse caused by excessive predation).
Only civilizations that evolve Higher Order Tao can break through the resource limits of their home planet and enter the stage of interstellar evolution.

### Two Paths Comparison

| Dimension | Lower Order Tao | Higher Order Tao |
|---|---|---|
| Core Logic | Spontaneous Predation | Active Symbiosis |
| Resource Allocation | One-way Concentration | Two-way Circulation |
| Class Mobility | Almost Stagnant | Opportunity Fairness |
| System Stability | Cyclical Collapse | Dynamic Balance |
| Innovation Vitality | Inhibited | Released |
| Long-term Evolution | Cyclical Repetition | Continuous Upgrade |

## 2.4 Core Clarification: Responding to Criticism and Logical Loop

To avoid misunderstanding, this theory makes the following core clarifications:

### Not Pseudoscience

This theory is a social philosophy analysis tool, not claiming physical-level truth, but only extracting laws from history and reality. "Axiom-Theorem" is a logical deduction tool, not natural science laws.

### Not Absolute Zero-Sum

Acknowledging that technology and collaboration can create increments, criticizing "increments being monopolized and plundered by the top layer", not denying increments themselves.

### Not Generalizing "Predation"

Predation only refers to: **relying on monopoly of resources/power to possess others' labor for free**; reasonable returns such as venture capital, management, and paid knowledge are not included.

### Higher Order Tao is Not Utopia

Only eliminating "internal human predation", obtaining resources from nature is a life instinct, the two are completely distinguished, and there is no logical contradiction.

### Not a Closed Faith

Verifiable: The collapse frequency of predatory groups and the survival quality of symbiotic groups can be tested through history and reality.

### Not Deifying History

Relevant practices are **pioneering attempts**, not the ultimate only truth, fully respecting the complexity and multifaceted nature of history.

## 2.5 One Sentence Summary

The Tao is **two evolutionary choices** for human groups under universal laws:

Either obey instincts and fall into the lower-order cycle of **predation-solidification-collapse**;
Or actively construct and move towards a higher-order civilization of **symbiosis-fairness-sustainability**.

It is not metaphysical destiny, not the law of the jungle, but a set of evolutionary logic that **sees clearly the essence of society, is implementable, and correctable**.
