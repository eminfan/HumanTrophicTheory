## 12.1 Higher Order Tao Theorem: The Possibility of Active Construction

### Core Proposition

**Higher Order Tao is an active construction aimed at the long-term survival of the group after recognizing the laws, and does not violate any underlying axioms.**

Higher Order Tao is not a static ultimate form that can be achieved once and for all, but an evolutionary practice process in which humans continuously trial and error, iterate, and dynamically optimize based on their deepening understanding of the objective laws of Tao. Its complete definition is:

**After thoroughly recognizing the cyclic fate of low-order predatory order, humans actively design and continuously optimize a symbiotic social order based on the core goal of "maximizing the long-term survival and evolutionary advantages of the group." It is not a perfect system built in one go, but a dynamic evolutionary process that is always in practice, always in cognition, and always in correction; its core vitality lies not in error-free top-level design, but in having an endogenous self-correction mechanism that always returns to the core goal.**

### Higher Order Tao Theorem System

#### 1. Compliance Theorem

**Higher Order Tao does not negate underlying laws such as survival needs, limited resources, and group evolution, but only reconstructs resource allocation and power rules.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Meta-axiom system
- **Core Logic**: Achieve better resource allocation through institutional design based on respect for objective laws
- **Practical Significance**: Avoid utopian fantasies and ensure the feasibility of the theory

#### 2. Anti-Predation Theorem

**Abolish "unpaid possession through monopoly of means of production/power" through institutions. There is no institutionalized predation within humanity, only equivalent exchange and reasonable division of labor benefits.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Predatory Profit-Seeking Axiom
- **Core Logic**: Contain predatory behavior through institutional design to ensure fair distribution of resources
- **Practical Significance**: Eliminate the internal root cause of the system and achieve sustainable development

#### 3. Group Advantage Theorem

**Incremental resources are invested in universal education, healthcare, innovation, infrastructure, and security. The overall vitality, risk resistance, and long-term survival quality are far higher than those of low-order order.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Collaborative Value-Added Axiom
- **Core Logic**: Improve the overall quality and capabilities of the group through public investment
- **Practical Significance**: Release the innovative potential of the group and enhance the overall competitiveness of the system

#### 4. Anti-Solidification Theorem

**Social mobility is based solely on individual ability, labor, and contribution, blocking intergenerational monopoly advantages and maintaining stable mobility.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Reverse design of the Social Solidification Theorem
- **Core Logic**: Establish a fair competition mechanism to ensure equal opportunities
- **Practical Significance**: Maintain social vitality and avoid system rigidity caused by social solidification

#### 5. Cycle-Breaking Theorem

**Eliminate the endogenous collapse driver of "unlimited escalation of predation," achieve sustainable resource allocation, and no longer fall into the cycle of low-order Tao.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Reverse design of the Endogenous Cycle Theorem
- **Core Logic**: Achieve balanced flow of energy through institutional design
- **Practical Significance**: Break through the historical cycle and achieve sustainable development of civilization

#### 6. Dynamic Iteration Theorem

**Human cognition of the objective laws of Tao always has phased limitations, and the external environment and internal structure of group evolution are always in dynamic change. Therefore, the practice of Higher Order Tao must follow the spiral upward law of "practice-cognition-deviation-correction-deepening of cognition-iterative upgrade." The long-term evolutionary advantage of Higher Order Tao does not depend on whether the system built in one go is perfect, but on whether it has an endogenous self-correction and self-revolution mechanism that can always adhere to the core goal of "maximizing the long-term survival and evolutionary advantages of the group" and continuously correct deviations in practice.**

- **Theoretical Basis**: Extension and expansion of the meta-axiom system
- **Core Logic**: Trial and error and correction are inevitable links in the practice of Higher Order Tao, and self-correction and self-iteration are its core vitality
- **Practical Significance**: Provide a theoretical basis for accommodating mistakes in historical practice and ensure the dynamic openness of the theory

### Key Distinction: Essential Goal vs. Practice Deviation

When understanding the historical practice of Higher Order Tao, it is necessary to clarify two core concepts that cannot be confused:

- **Essential Goal of Higher Order Tao**: Centered on the long-term survival of the gene group, achieve zero institutionalized predation, universal resource benefit, and maximized group collaboration efficiency, and completely break away from the historical cycle of low-order Tao. This goal conforms to the meta-axiom system of Tao and is objective, stable, and unshakable.

- **Behavioral Deviation in the Practice Process of Higher Order Tao**: In the process of exploring the path to achieve the core goal, behaviors that violate the core axioms of Tao and deviate from the core goal due to cognitive limitations, environmental constraints, and lack of experience. This is phased and correctable, and does not represent the failure of the theoretical essence of Higher Order Tao.

### Contemporary Practice

#### Socialism with Chinese Characteristics: Correction and Iteration of Higher Order Tao

The practice of socialism with Chinese characteristics since the reform and opening up in 1978 is essentially the self-correction and iterative upgrade of Higher Order Tao. Its core logic is to address the four major deviations in exploration, return to the core axioms of Tao one by one, re-anchor the core goal of "maximizing the long-term survival and evolutionary advantages of the group," and form a practical path of Higher Order Tao that is implementable, verifiable, and has endogenous iterative capabilities.

1. **Return to the Ontology of Anti-Entropy Survival: Focus on Economic Construction to Consolidate the Material Basis for Group Evolution**

In response to the deviation of "violating the axiom of limited resources in time and space and breaking through objective boundaries with subjectivism," the core correction of reform and opening up is to completely abandon the wrong line of "taking class struggle as the key link," establish the basic line of "focusing on economic construction," and return to the ultimate ontology of Tao that "survival is anti-entropy." Through the household contract responsibility system in rural areas, the reform of state-owned enterprises in cities, and the construction of the socialist market economic system, we fully respect the objective laws of economic development and improve anti-entropy efficiency through division of labor, collaboration, and institutional innovation within resource boundaries.

2. **Reconstruct the Meme Adaptation System: The Proposition of the Primary Stage of Socialism to Achieve Precise Matching of Memes and Evolutionary Stages**

In response to the deviation of "premature construction of meme system and separation from the realistic stage of group evolution," the core correction of socialism with Chinese characteristics is to put forward the core proposition that "China is in and will long remain in the primary stage of socialism," and reconstruct a meme system that precisely matches the evolutionary stage of the group. Based on this core proposition, the theoretical system of socialism with Chinese characteristics has been formed, realizing the continuous iteration of the meme system: from Deng Xiaoping Theory, the important thought of "Three Represents," the Scientific Outlook on Development, to Xi Jinping Thought on Socialism with Chinese Characteristics for a New Era, the meme system has always adapted to the stage of productive forces development and the evolutionary needs of the group.

3. **Construct a Closed Loop of Power Constraint: Legal System and Self-Revolution to Contain Power Alienation and Predation Regeneration**

In response to the deviation of "ignoring the endogenous risk of power alienation and lack of institutionalized constraint mechanisms," the core correction of socialism with Chinese characteristics is to construct a comprehensive power constraint closed loop of "legal constraint + democratic supervision + self-revolution":
- Establish the basic strategy of "comprehensively governing the country by law" and regulate power operation with rigid laws
- Establish a comprehensive supervision system to achieve full-process and full-coverage supervision of power operation
- Take the Party's self-revolution as the second answer to breaking the historical cycle and promote the fight against corruption

4. **Reshape the Symbiotic Balance between Group and Individual: Dynamic Unity of Efficiency and Fairness to Activate Individual Vitality and Group Advantages**

In response to the deviation of "imbalance in group-individual symbiosis and neglect of individual legitimate rights and interests," the core correction of socialism with Chinese characteristics is to achieve the "dynamic unity of efficiency and fairness":
- Activate individual vitality: Recognize and protect individual legitimate property rights and operational autonomy through market economic system reform
- Adhere to group orientation: Ensure that the fruits of development benefit all people through poverty alleviation and the construction of social security system
- Dynamic balance mechanism: Always dynamically adjust the balance between efficiency and fairness according to the realistic needs of group evolution

- **Features**: Public ownership as the main body, diverse forms of ownership developing together
- **Achievements**: Rapid economic development and historic achievements in poverty alleviation
- **Exploration**: Innovative practice in the construction of symbiotic order
