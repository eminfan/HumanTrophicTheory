# Chapter 3 Human Nature: Dual Coding of Genes and Memes

## 3.4 Derivative Mechanisms of Dual Coding: Instinct, Domestication and Game

### Core Proposition

**The interaction between genes (instinct) and memes (culture) is not always harmonious symbiosis, but a continuous dynamic game within the individual. This game derives three core mechanisms of human society: Desire Domestication, Cultural Mutation, and System Immunity.**

---

### I. Desire Domestication Mechanism (Instant Feedback vs Delayed Gratification)

#### 1.1 Source of Conflict

- **Gene Logic (Instinct)**: Pursuing **Instant Feedback**.
    - Mechanism: Dopamine reward circuit.
    - Goal: Ensure immediate survival and reproduction (e.g., eat when seeing sugar, chase when seeing opposite sex).
- **Meme Logic (Culture)**: Pursuing **Delayed Gratification**.
    - Mechanism: Inhibitory control of the prefrontal cortex.
    - Goal: Ensure long-term interests of the group (e.g., farming requires waiting for harvest, saving for the future).

#### 1.2 Domestication Process

Memes domesticate genes by **reconstructing the reward circuit**:

1.  **Suppression**: Inhibiting instinctual impulses through moral shame (Cortisol/Stress Hormone).
    - *Case*: Fasting, abstinence, asceticism.
2.  **Transfer**: Transferring the trigger point of dopamine from "physiological satisfaction" to "social recognition".
    - *Case*: Getting a medal brings more pleasure than getting food.
3.  **Alienation (Modern Disease)**:
    - Commercial memes (consumerism) reverse-exploit gene instincts through **Supernormal Stimuli**.
    - *Case*: Short videos, high-sugar foods, pornography industry. They bypass meme censorship and directly bombard the primitive receptors of genes, leading to **"Meme Rebellion"**.

---

### II. Cultural Mutation Mechanism (Conservative Inheritance vs Radical Innovation)

#### 2.1 Mismatch in Evolutionary Rates

- **Gene Evolution**: Extremely slow (ten-thousand-year scale). Tends to be **conservative**, maintaining physiological homeostasis.
- **Meme Evolution**: Extremely fast (ten-year scale). Tends to be **radical**, trying new strategies.

#### 2.2 Double-Edged Sword of Mutation

1.  **Adaptive Innovation**: Meme mutation helps genes break through environmental limits.
    - *Case*: Cold-resistant gene evolution takes tens of thousands of years, but the meme of "sewing fur" takes only one generation to allow tropical naked apes to survive in the Ice Age.
2.  **Adaptive Trap**: Meme mutation speed is too fast, causing genes to fail to adapt.
    - *Case*: High-carbohydrate diet (meme) brought by agricultural civilization led to diabetes and obesity (gene maladaptation).
    - *Case*: Atomized life in modern cities (meme) led to widespread loneliness and depression (social gene maladaptation).

---

### III. System Immunity Mechanism (Entropy Reduction Maintenance vs Individual Clearance)

#### 3.1 System's Low Entropy Demand

As a dissipative structure, in order to maintain overall order (low entropy), the social system must clear internal "chaos factors". In the eyes of the system, **individuals who do not obey meme domestication are "cancer cells"**.

#### 3.2 Core Indicators: Parasitism and Division

The system identifies "cancer cells" through two major indicators:

1.  **Parasitism Coefficient (Pc)**:
    - Definition: Individuals or classes that occupy resources but do not contribute negative entropy.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Senescent cells that consume energy but do not work.
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Rentier class, sluggards, overly bloated bureaucracy.
2.  **Division Index (Sd)**:
    - Definition: The degree to which identifying with different meme systems leads to a decline in group collaboration efficiency.
    - *Biological Analogy*: Autoimmune disease (immune system attacks own cells).
    - *Sociological Manifestation*: Religious conflicts, partisan polarization, separatism.

#### 3.3 Clearance and Discipline

When **System Risk (R) ∝ Pc × Sd** exceeds the threshold, the system will trigger an immune response:

- **Gentle Means**: Educational assimilation, public opinion isolation, marginalization.
- **Violent Means**: Imprisonment, exile, war cleansing.

**Lingshi Insight**:
> So-called "justice" is often the **antibody** of the system's immune response; and so-called "heresy" is often the **antigen** the system tries to clear.
> This passage is a typical application of **Lingshi Analysis** in the social system immune mechanism, aiming to **demystify and de-moralize "justice" and "heresy" through biological analogy**.

### **Core Metaphor**

*   **Social System ≈ Biological Organism**: To maintain its own orderly (low entropy) survival, the system must identify "us" and "enemies".
*   **Justice ≈ Antibody**: Not absolute good, but a **tool** created by the system to identify, mark, and clear threats.
*   **Heresy ≈ Antigen**: Not absolute evil, but any **foreign object** that is **incompatible** with the existing system logic and may cause system division (Sd increase) or resource loss (Pc increase).

### **Deep Analysis**

#### **1. Functional Essence of Justice (Antibody)**
In conventional narratives, "justice" represents the sacred, correct, and moral high ground.
But from the perspective of **The Way Theory**, "justice" is the **immune identification code** evolved by the system.
*   **Function**: When a behavior is marked as "unjust", all individuals (white blood cells) in the system will be mobilized to attack it.
*   **Relativity**: Just as antibodies differ across species, "justice" in different social systems is completely different, or even opposite.
    *   *Case*: In Sparta, infanticide was "justice" (for the strength of the population); in modern society, infanticide is a "heinous crime". The content of justice has changed, but **the function of "maintaining system survival" has not changed**.

#### **2. Structural Essence of Heresy (Antigen)**
"Heretics" are often not because they are evil, but because they **trigger the system's rejection reaction**.
*   **Type A: Meme Division (Thought Antigen)**
    *   If a thought (such as heliocentrism) challenges the core legitimacy narrative of the system (such as geocentric theocracy), it will cause the system's **Division Index (Sd)** to soar, and the system must clear it like a virus.
*   **Type B: Resource Parasitism (Material Antigen)**
    *   If a group (such as refugees or monopoly oligarchs) only consumes resources and does not contribute order, causing the **Parasitism Coefficient (Pc)** to be too high, the system will try to expel or destroy it.

### **Summary**

The purpose of this passage is to let you **jump out of the trap of moral judgment** and look at history with a cold system perspective:

> When you see a movement of "justice" judging "heresy", do not rush to take sides.
> You may just be seeing a **social system immune response**—a huge biological organism is having a fever, trying to burn the virus in its body. As for who is good and who is evil, that is just the **mobilization password** written by the immune system to mobilize white blood cells (the masses).

### Chapter Summary

Humans are not only carriers of genes, but also hosts of memes.
- **Desire Domestication** makes us sacrifice the present for the future.
- **Cultural Mutation** makes us sacrifice stability for adaptation.
- **System Immunity** makes us sacrifice individuals for the collective.

These three major mechanisms together constitute the underlying logic of the complex operation of human society.
